Site Plan

Site Name

Sara Mangas Creaciones

This is the name of the business that I have with my wife Sara; and represents the spirit of what we do: all kind of digital print, stationery and vinyl creations that make your imagination a reality.

Site Purpose

To provide information of the various products in our catalogue, share our contact info at the same time that we raise awereness of our social media and prescence in google; and to provide a subscription to a newsletter that will provide news about our seasonal products, sales and special promo.

Scenarios

  • What type of creations or products you have?
  • What sizes of shirts do you have?
  • Where can I find contact information to order a product?

Color Schema

  • First color; for header, footer and headings: rgb(250, 176, 214)
  • Second color; for div, sections, parragraphs and text background in general: rgb(128, 208, 205)
  • Third color; as contrast and main background: rgb(255, 255, 255)
